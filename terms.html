1. Who we are and what we do

FreeJobTracker.ai ("we", "us", "our") is a free AI-powered job application tracking service operated from India. Our service connects to your email inbox via read-only OAuth authentication and uses artificial intelligence to automatically detect and classify job-related emails into five categories: Applied, Screening, Interview, Rejected, and Offer.

We store only the extracted metadata from your emails — company name, job title, application status, and date — to populate your job tracking dashboard. We never store email content. The service is free to use and requires no manual data entry.
