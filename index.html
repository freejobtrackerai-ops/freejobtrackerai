Our promise
We read.
We classify.
We forget.

Your inbox is yours. We access it only to detect job-related emails. We classify them using AI. We show you the results. We do not store, index, sell, or train on your email content.

Read-only OAuth access. We can never send, delete, or modify any email in your inbox. Ever.
No email storage. Email content is processed in real-time and discarded. We store only extracted metadata — company, role, status, date.
No AI training on your data. Your emails are never used to train AI models, including ours.
Revoke anytime. Disconnect from Google's security dashboard in two clicks. All your data is deleted within 24 hours.
No selling. No sharing. We are not an ad company. Your data is never sold to recruiters, employers, or third parties.
