Quick answers
Is FreeJobTracker.ai really free?
Yes, completely. No credit card, no trial period, no hidden fees. FreeJobTracker.ai is free to use forever.
Can you read all my emails?
We request read-only Gmail access and scan only for job-related emails. We never store email content — only metadata like company name, role, and status.
How do I disconnect my Gmail?
Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions, find FreeJobTracker.ai, and click Remove Access. Your data is deleted from our servers within 24 hours.
Which email providers are supported?
Gmail is available now. Outlook, Yahoo Mail, and IMAP providers are coming soon. We'll notify you when they launch.
My application was classified incorrectly.
AI classification isn't perfect yet. Please use the form above to report the issue and we'll use your feedback to improve accuracy.
