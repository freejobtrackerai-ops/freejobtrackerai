Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

FreeJobTracker.ai connects to your Gmail inbox via read-only OAuth to automatically detect and classify job-related emails. We never store email content, sell your data, or use it for advertising or AI training.

1. What information do we collect?

Personal information you disclose to us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register on the Services or contact us. This may include your name and email address.

Sensitive information — Email content

We access your Gmail inbox via read-only OAuth for the sole purpose of detecting and classifying job-related emails. We use a two-stage process:

  • Stage 1: We analyse only email subject lines, sender names, and snippet previews to determine whether an email is job-related.
  • Stage 2: For confirmed job-related emails only, we read the full email body to classify it. After classification, all email content is immediately discarded from memory.

Google API Compliance

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use Gmail data for advertising or building user profiles.

2. How do we process your information?

We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our Services. For confirmed job-related emails, we transmit content to our AI provider (Anthropic) for real-time classification. Anthropic does not retain or train on this data.

3. Sharing your information

We share information only with necessary service providers: Anthropic (AI Classification), Supabase (Secure Database for metadata), and Google (Authentication).
