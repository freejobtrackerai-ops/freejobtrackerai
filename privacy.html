Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FreeJobTracker.ai collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our services.

1. Google User Data Policy Compliance

FreeJobTracker.ai's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Data Collection

We access your Gmail messages (Read-Only) solely to identify job-related correspondence. We do not store the content of your emails. We only store metadata such as company names and application statuses.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is processed through Anthropic's Claude API for classification purposes only and is not used for AI training by third parties.
