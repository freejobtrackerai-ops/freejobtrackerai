Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FreeJobTracker.ai ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services at https://freejobtracker.ai.

FreeJobTracker.ai connects to your Gmail inbox via read-only OAuth to automatically detect and classify job-related emails. We never store email content, sell your data, or use it for advertising or AI training.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal information you disclose to us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register on the Services or contact us. This may include your name and email address.

Sensitive information - Email content

We access your Gmail inbox via read-only OAuth for the sole purpose of detecting and classifying job-related emails. We use a two-stage process:

  • Stage 1 (Pre-filter): We analyse only email subject lines, sender names, and snippet previews to determine whether an email is job-related. Non-job emails are immediately discarded and their content is never accessed further or sent to any AI.
  • Stage 2 (Classification): For confirmed job-related emails only, we read the full email body and send it to our AI provider (Anthropic) to classify it as Applied, Screening, Interview, Rejected, or Offer. After classification, all email content is immediately discarded from memory. It is never stored, logged, or retained on our servers.

We store only four metadata fields: company name, job title, classification status, and date. No email body content, subject lines, or snippets are ever stored in our database.

Information automatically collected

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our Services, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage data.

Google API Compliance

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We access Gmail data solely to provide the job tracking service. We do not use Gmail data for advertising, to build user profiles, or for any purpose not disclosed in this Privacy Policy.

  • Two-stage email classification. We use the two-stage process described in Section 1. All email content is discarded after classification. We store only: company name, job title, classification status, and date.

4. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Information?

We share information only with the following third parties for processing purposes:

  • Anthropic (AI Classification Provider). For confirmed job-related emails only, we transmit email subject lines, sender names, and full email body content to Anthropic's Claude API to return a classification label. Anthropic does not retain or train on this data. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
  • Supabase (Database Provider). We store user account information and classified job metadata (company name, job title, status, date) in Supabase's secure PostgreSQL database. Email content is never stored.
  • Google (Authentication and Gmail API). We use Google OAuth for authentication and the Gmail API to access your inbox with read-only permission.
  • Business Transfers. We may share or transfer your information in connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition of our business.

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to any other third parties for advertising, marketing, or commercial purposes.

7. Do We Offer Artificial Intelligence-Based Products?

Yes. FreeJobTracker.ai uses artificial intelligence to automatically classify job-related emails into five categories: Applied, Screening, Interview, Rejected, and Offer.

Two-stage classification process

  • Stage 1 - Job detection. We analyse only the email subject line, sender name, and snippet preview to determine whether an email is job-related. Non-job emails are discarded immediately and full content is never accessed.
  • Stage 2 - Category classification. Only emails confirmed as job-related in Stage 1 proceed to this stage. We read the full email body and send it to Anthropic's Claude API to classify it. Once the label is returned, all email content is immediately discarded from memory and never stored.

What data is sent to our AI provider

For Stage 2 classification, we transmit to Anthropic's Claude API: email subject line, sender name and email address, and full email body (for confirmed job-related emails only).

Data retention by our AI provider

Anthropic processes email content solely to return a classification label and does not retain or train on this data.

What we store after classification

After classification, we store only: company name, job title, classification status, and date. Email body content, subject lines, and snippets are never written to our database.

How to opt out
