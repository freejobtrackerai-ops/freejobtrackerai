7. Do we offer artificial intelligence-based products?

Yes. FreeJobTracker.ai uses artificial intelligence to automatically classify job-related emails into five categories: Applied, Screening, Interview, Rejected, and Offer.

Two-stage classification process

We use a two-stage approach specifically designed to minimise the amount of email content accessed:

  • Stage 1 — Job detection. We analyse only the email subject line, sender name, and snippet preview to determine whether an email is job-related. For the vast majority of emails — newsletters, personal messages, receipts — the process stops here and full content is never accessed.
  • Stage 2 — Category classification. Only emails confirmed as job-related in Stage 1 proceed to this stage. We read the full email body and send it to Anthropic's Claude API to classify it into one of the five categories above. Once the classification label is returned, all email content is immediately discarded from memory.

What data is sent to our AI provider

For Stage 2 classification, we transmit the following to Anthropic's Claude API:

  • Email subject line
  • Sender name and email address
  • Full email body (for confirmed job-related emails only)

We do not transmit your name, Google account details, or any information beyond the email content itself.

Data retention by our AI provider

Anthropic processes email content solely to return a classification label. Under our usage of their API, Anthropic does not retain or train on this data beyond the scope of completing the API request. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for full details of their data handling practices.

What we store after classification

After classification, we store only the following in our database:

  • Company name (extracted from sender)
  • Job title (extracted from subject line)
  • Classification status (Applied, Screening, Interview, Rejected, or Offer)
  • Date of the email

Email body content, subject lines, and snippets are never written to our database. They exist only transiently in memory during the classification process.

How to opt out

You can disconnect your Gmail inbox at any time from your account settings or by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing FreeJobTracker.ai. All stored metadata will be deleted within 24 hours of disconnection.
